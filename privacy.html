Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Introduction

Cloud Motion Lab ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Rock Identifier mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Camera Images: Rock Identifier uses your device camera to capture images of rocks and minerals for identification. Images are processed on-device using machine learning and may be sent to our classification API for analysis. We do not store your images on our servers after processing is complete.

Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data through TelemetryDeck to understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This data does not include personally identifiable information.

Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is handled by Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details.

Data Storage

Your rock collection data (identified rocks, scan history) is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions and purchases. Privacy Policy
  • TelemetryDeck: Anonymous analytics. Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Rock Identifier is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can delete your local rock collection data at any time by deleting the app. To manage your subscription, visit your Apple ID settings.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
